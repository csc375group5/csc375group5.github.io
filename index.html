Welcome to Fifth Group, Inc.

We are a IT Consultancy, specializing in change management and system analysis.

We are Group 5, an organization dedicated to the creation of reliable, useful systems for a complex world. Group 5 is a Victoria based organization with a global presence, and our team members have the expertise you need to create the system you didn't know was possible. What is the Group 5 difference? We don't just build systems for you, we build them with you. Group 5 understands the need for stakeholder involvement in the system design process: we meet face to face with all interested parties and innovate the solution that's right for your business. So go ahead, contact us, and discover the Group 5 difference.

Our Client

Our client CARSA(Centre for Athletics, Recreation and Special Abilities) is the new University of Victoria recreation centre. It contains a wide range of training facilities for staff and students including weight rooms, sports equipment and a rock climbing wall. CARSA opened in May 2015 and since the beginning of semester it has been extremely popular among students.

The CARSA Project

CARSA has requested our expertise in system analysis and design in order to improve their ability to provide a premium registration experience to CARSA members. The explosive popularity of CARSA since its opening has led to problems with too many new customers trying to register for membership at the same time, overwhelming the staff.
